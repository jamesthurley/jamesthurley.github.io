Off The Wall is an app for Kilter Board users who want to have fun with the wall between climbs. It connects to your Kilter training board over Bluetooth and lets you draw pictures, create animations, and play games using the Kilter Board as a giant screen.

- Draw pictures on the Kilter Board in real time. Import photos and display them on the board. Save and load your pictures locally.

- Sequence multiple pictures together to create animations and play them on the Kilter Board. Save and load your animations locally.

- Play simple games live on the Kilter Board, such as the 70s classic "Pong". More games coming soon.

### Free and private
Off The Wall does not require an account, does not include ads, and does not collect or transmit personal data.

Supported boards:
- Kilter Board Homewall
- Kilter Board Original

More boards may follow in the future.

Requires Bluetooth Low Energy and a compatible Aurora/Kilter controller for
board control. The app can still be opened and used without a board.


---

## Limitations

This app has not been tested on all Kilter Board configurations and hardware.
Board configurations which have not been tested are marked as "Unverified".
If you do manage to test the app on an Unverified configuration [let me know](/offthewall/support)
if it works or not so that I can remove the "Unverified" flag.

The Safari browser does not support Web Bluetooth, so you will have to use the iOS App if you are on an Apple device.
