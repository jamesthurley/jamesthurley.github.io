<div class="otw-commercial-hero">
  <p class="otw-kicker">Commercial Gyms</p>
  <h2>Put your Kilter Board to work between climbs</h2>
  <p class="otw-lede">Your Kilter Board is already one of the most eye-catching features in the gym. Off The Wall makes it just as useful when no one is climbing.</p>
  <p>Turn quiet time into a branded display, create atmosphere for a special event, and stop a forgotten climb from glowing for hours. Choose the content once, then use the mode that fits the moment.</p>
</div>

<div class="otw-highlight">
  <p class="otw-kicker">One playlist, two modes</p>
  <h3>Your wall, your content</h3>
  <p>Build a playlist from your logo, artwork, animations, and ambiance effects. Use the playlist for everyday downtime or an uninterrupted event display.</p>
</div>

<div class="otw-card-grid otw-mode-grid">
  <article class="otw-card">
    <p class="otw-card-kicker otw-green">Idle Mode</p>
    <h3>Catch attention, then save the LEDs</h3>
    <p>Idle Mode quietly watches for an unused board. After your chosen delay, it brings the wall to life with your playlist, plays for as long as you choose, then clears every hold and returns to monitoring.</p>
    <p>The moment a climber connects, Off The Wall automatically steps aside. They get the board; you get an unattended display and prolong the life of your LEDs at the same time.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="otw-card">
    <p class="otw-card-kicker otw-warm">Event Mode</p>
    <h3>Make the wall part of the event</h3>
    <p>When the board is deliberately out of climbing service during an event, Event Mode starts your playlist immediately and keeps control of the connection.</p>
    <p>Your logo, sponsor artwork, animations, or ambient effects stay on the wall for the duration you set, actively preventing another connection replacing the display halfway through the night.</p>
  </article>
</div>

<div class="otw-card-grid otw-benefit-grid">
  <article class="otw-card"><p class="otw-card-kicker otw-green">Protect your investment</p><h3>Clear the last climb automatically</h3><p>Avoid leaving the final problem of the day lit for hours. Idle Mode gives the wall a purposeful display, then switches every hold off to reduce unnecessary LED time.</p></article>
  <article class="otw-card"><p class="otw-card-kicker otw-green">Climber friendly</p><h3>Automation without interruption</h3><p>Idle Mode only takes over when the wall is free and yields automatically when someone wants to climb. No front-desk intervention required.</p></article>
  <article class="otw-card"><p class="otw-card-kicker otw-warm">Wall-scale display</p><h3>Branding that cannot be missed</h3><p>Turn hundreds of board LEDs into a venue logo, sponsor moment, or moving backdrop that can be seen from across the gym.</p></article>
</div>

<div class="otw-steps">
  <div><p class="otw-kicker">Simple to run</p><h3>Set it up in three steps</h3></div>
  <ol>
    <li><strong>Build your playlist.</strong> Add a logo, saved artwork, animation, or ambiance effect.</li>
    <li><strong>Choose the behavior.</strong> Use Idle Mode for normal climbing or Event Mode when the wall is a display.</li>
    <li><strong>Set it running.</strong> Works with your existing wall without any modifications.</li>
  </ol>
</div>

<div class="otw-commercial-pricing">
  <p class="otw-kicker">Pricing</p>
  <h2>Low-cost subscription</h2>
  <p>Commercial Gym modes require a low-cost subscription, which can be cancelled at any time. Used correctly, Idle Mode may prolong the life of your wall's LEDs, making it an inexpensive investment in the future of your Kilter Board.</p>
  <p>For reliable day-to-day monitoring, keep a dedicated device plugged in near the board.</p>
</div>
